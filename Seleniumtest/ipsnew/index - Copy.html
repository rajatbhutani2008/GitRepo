Indraprasth Public School

Welcome

Education is a process that begins in childhood and lasts a lifetime. We endeavour to create the spirit for lifelong learning.

Indraprasth Public School is dedicated to empowering students to become life-long learners, committed to Honesty, Equality, Respect, Integrity, Trust, Achievement, Generosity and Excellence. A rigorous and innovative curriculum will be taught within small learning communities and career academies, enhanced with extra-curricular activities, to create productive citizens for a global society.

Our Mission

The motto of Indraprasth Public School is "Vidya Dadaati Vinayam" from knowledge comes modesty.

The mission of Indraprasth Public School is to provide each student a diverse education in a safe, supportive environment that promotes sound values, self discipline, motivation and excellence in learning. The school joins with parents and community to assist the students in developing skills to become independent self sufficient adults who will succeed and contribute responsibly in a global community.

Our Values

Our values are at the heart of our teaching practices and define the standards of ethical conduct we foster in our children.

At Indraprasth Public School, we believe in and abide by a set of core values that govern, and reflect in each of our actions. The principal values of our school community are: Act with Integrity, Uncompromising quality , Compassion and Innovation, Take ownership and Responsibility, Lifetime Learning, Commitment to Excellence.
